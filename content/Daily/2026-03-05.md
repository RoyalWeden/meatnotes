bruised reed & smoking flax:

> Isaiah 43:16-17
>
> 16 Thus saith the Lord, who makes a way in the sea, and a path in the mighty water;
>
> 17 who brought forth chariots and horse, and a mighty multitude: but they have lain down, and shall not rise: they are extinct, as quenched flax. 

- quenched flax = fire that is about put out
  - a quenched wick/candle
    - foolish virgins that are sleeping w/o candle burning 

> Isaiah 42:3 A bruised reed shall he not break, and the smoking flax shall he not quench: he shall bring forth judgment unto truth.

- reed/rod & flax/wick/candle = judgment unto truth

smoking = wax dim (strongs)
